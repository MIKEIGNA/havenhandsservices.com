What does the placement fee cover?

The one-time placement fee covers the entire recruitment, multi-level background checks, professional module training costs, candidate sourcing, coordination of interviews, contract guidance, and initial integration support.

Do you take commission from the caregiver's salary?

No. We believe in ethical placement. 100% of the salary you agree upon goes directly to your caregiver. We do not deduct any monthly commission cuts or cuts from their hard-earned salary.

What is your replacement policy?

With our Gold package, we offer a free replacement guarantee within the first 3 months of placement. If the match is not successful for any reason, we will match you with another candidate at no additional cost.
