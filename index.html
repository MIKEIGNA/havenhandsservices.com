Common Questions

Answers for Families

How does Haven Hands vet its caregivers and nannies?

Every caregiver undergoes a rigorous multi-level background check including criminal record verification, character reference checks, past employment verification, and a deep ethical interview. We don't compromise on safety.

What training do Haven Hands caregivers receive?

Our 10-module professional training covers: Professional Communication, Housekeeping & Home Management, Childcare Basics, Digital Literacy, First Aid & Safety, Food Handling & Kitchen Hygiene, Appliance Operation, Laundry & Garment Care, Mindset & Professionalism, and Financial Literacy.

How long does the hiring process take?

Most families are matched with the perfect caregiver within 3 to 7 days after the initial consultation. We present 2–3 carefully selected candidates for you to interview before making your final choice.

What happens if I'm not happy with the caregiver?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If the placement isn't working out, we'll work with you to find a suitable replacement. We offer ongoing support to ensure a successful long-term match.

Do you serve areas outside Nairobi?

Our primary service area is Nairobi and its environs. However, we also serve families in Mombasa, Kisumu, and other major Kenyan cities. Contact us to discuss your specific location.
