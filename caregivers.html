Before You Apply

Do you qualify?

Check if you meet the basic requirements. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we'll guide you through the process.

Requirements

  • Valid National ID or Passport
  • Registration fee: KES 500 (non-refundable)
  • Willingness to undergo 2-week training
  • At least 1 reference from previous employer
  • Commitment to ethical work standards
  • Good communication skills (English or Kiswahili)
